About the Oakcrest Manor Nursing Home

This state-of-the-art facility is located in Austin. In order to achieve each individual's medical goals, Oakcrest Nursing and Rehab Center coordinates many services which contribute to their quality of life, such as dental, optometric, audiology, pharmacological, dietary, social and psychological services. These services, and the professionals on the team, are key in developing a comprehensive medical plan. Special attention is given to developing a caring and qualified nursing team in our facility.
