St Francis Nursing Home
630 W Woodlawn Ave, San Antonio, TX 78212

Description:

St. Francis Nursing Home is a nonprofit, locally owned and operated by the Seraphic Sisters, dedicated to following the teachings of St. Francis of Assisi. Since 1970, we have been caring for the sick, poor, and elderly of San Antonio. We proudly address the holistic needs of the aged, individuals in need, and their families. At St. Francis, we are committed to providing compassionate and loving care to our elderly residents, deeply rooted in the teachings and values of our Catholic faith. Our mission is to ensure that every individual in our care experiences dignity, respect, and a sense of belonging, regardless of their sex, race, financial situation or religion.
